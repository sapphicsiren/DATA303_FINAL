Bakery Earnings Trends


Full Monthly Earnings from Deliveries

This visualization shows the total earnings made from deliveries during each month. For the sake of visual cohesion, I chose to exclude the data for July 2019 and May 2020. This is because the data collection period spanned from July 11, 2019 to May 2, 2020, therefore less than a full month's of sales were made during these times.

Notably, February and March 2020 had the highest earnings.

Every Transaction on the Highest Earning Day

This visualization contains information on every sale made during the highest earning day (July 26, 2019). Each unique transaction is its own bar which is then split by specific item bought.

We can clearly see that on this day there quite a few customers that purchased over 5 goods, with one transaction totaling to 31 items!
