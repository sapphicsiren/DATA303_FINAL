Bakery Trends Over Time


Daily Delivery Sales: Split by Hour

This visualization displays every day of sales split by hour. The scale on the x-axis goes from 10:00 to 18:00 (10 AM to 6 PM). As these are delivery sales, it is possible for customers to place orders outside of the bakery's opening hours.

Notably, there were two orders during the entirety of the data collection period that were placed after 18:00 (6 PM). The first was made on October 27, 2019 at 23:02 (11:02 PM) and was an order for seven Angbutter. The second was on February 19, 2020 as 22:13 (10:13 PM) and was an order for one Angbutter.






Total Monthly Sales: Split by Day of Week

This visualization contains the monthly sales data split by day of week. Here, we can clearly see that orders are seldom placed on Tuesday. The reason for this is that, according to the data's author, the bakery is usually closed on Tuesdays.

It should also be noted that there are observably less sales in July 2019 and May 2020. This is because the data collection period spanned from July 11, 2019 to May 2, 2020, therefore the the end months did not have full month of data collection.
