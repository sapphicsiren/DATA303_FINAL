General Sales Trends


Raw Counts of Each Type of Item Offered

This visualization shows a breakdown of the items sold by the bakery. Each marker represents one item on the bakery's menu.

Visually, we can see that most of the items offered are sweet pastries.






Daily Average Count of Bakery Goods Sold

This visualization shows the average count of goods sold every day. It is clear that the most popular product sold is the "Angbutter," a savory pastry consisting of butter and red bean paste in bread.

Interstingly, during the entire span of the data collection, no customers ordered "Mad Garlic" or "Croque Monsieur" for delivery.






Weekly Average Count of Bakery Goods Sold

This visualization shows the average count of goods sold by week day. We can see that sales are generally consistent during the week, though there are noticably less on Tuesday. The reasoning behind this can be found in the original Kaggle post, where the author states that the bakery is usually closed on Tuesdays.
